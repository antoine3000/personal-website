Video compression

ffmpeg -i video.mp4 -b 1000000 compressed-video.mp4 or ffmpeg -i video.mp4 -b 1000000 -t 10 compressed-video.mp4 where -t 10 is the duration (10 sec.)

Imagemagick recipes
